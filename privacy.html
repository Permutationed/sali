Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Achieva. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our services to track and share your goals.

2. Information We Collect

Achieva collects certain types of information to provide and improve our services. This data may be linked to your identity or used in an anonymized way.

person Data Linked to You

  • Contact Info: Name, Email Address, Phone Number.
  • User Content: Other User Content (goals, progress), Photos or Videos (profile/goal images).

visibility_off Data Not Linked to You

  • User Content: Aggregated or anonymized user interaction data.

Specifically, the following data types are collected from the app:

  • Contact Information: Name, Email Address, Phone Number.
  • User Content: Goals, progress, habits, and "Other User Content" you create.
  • Media: Photos or Videos you may upload (e.g., profile pictures).
  • Communications: Data related to Emails or Text Messages sent through the platform's features.

3. How Your Data is Used

Your data is used to maintain your account, sync your goals across devices, and enable social features. We do not sell your personal information or contact details to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

All collected data is stored in our secure, encrypted database. We take industry-standard precautions to ensure the safety of your information, though no system is perfectly secure.
