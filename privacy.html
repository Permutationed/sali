Privacy Policy

Data Not Collected

Our Commitment

At Achieva, your privacy is our top priority. We believe that your goals and progress are your business, and ours only to help you track them locally on your device.

1. Data Collection

The developer does not collect any data from this app. We do not track your usage, we do not store your goals on our servers, and we do not have access to any personal information through your use of the Achieva app.

2. Local Storage

All data you input into Achieva (such as goals, habits, and progress) is stored strictly on your local device. This data never leaves your phone unless you choose to share it through system-level sharing features.

3. Third-Party Services

Since we do not collect data, we do not share any data with third-party services. There are no analytics, no advertising trackers, and no hidden data harvesting in Achieva.

4. Changes to This Policy

If we ever decide to change how Achieva works in a way that requires data collection, we will update this policy and notify users clearly within the app. For now, we are proud to be a data-free application.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about our "Data Not Collected" policy, you may email us at privacy@achieva.com.
