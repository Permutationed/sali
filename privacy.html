Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Achieva. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our services to track and share your goals.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with a seamless experience across devices, we collect and store the following information in our secure cloud database:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your email address and profile details to manage your access.
  • Goal Data: The goals you create, your progress updates, streaks, and habits are stored on our servers so you can access them from any device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect basic device information (IP address, OS version) to ensure the stability and security of our services.

3. How Your Data is Stored

Your goals and progress are stored in a secure, encrypted database on our servers. This allows us to sync your data in real-time and enable social features like sharing progress with friends.

4. Data Sharing & Privacy

We do not sell your personal data or goal progress to third parties. Your data is only shared with other users (like your friends on Achieva) based on the privacy settings you choose within the app.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data stored in our database. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about how we store your data, please contact us at privacy@achieva.com.
